How it works

Three steps
01

Pump

Pump the unit 10 to 20 times in a row to create a powerful vacuum. Continuous pumping isn't required.

02

Extract

The vacuum draws the old oil up through the dipstick tube and into the see-through, calibrated container.

03

Pour & recycle

Empty the captured oil with the pour spout, then take the used oil to a recycling depot.
